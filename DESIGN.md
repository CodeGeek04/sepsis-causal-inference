# Design Direction

## Scene

A researcher is reading a printed methods paper at a large desk in bright daylight, with notes, translucent overlays, and hand-drawn causal sketches spread across the surface.

## Color Strategy

Committed. Warm paper-tinted neutrals carry most of the surface, with precise teal, rust, and ink accents to differentiate data, treatment, notes, and outcomes.

## Typography

- Display: serif with editorial weight and tension
- Body: highly legible sans
- Numeric and method labels: compact mono

## Interaction Principles

- Every major concept gets at least one interactive visual or controllable example.
- Motion should reveal relationships, not decorate.
- Scroll should feel like moving through a laboratory notebook.

## Components

- Narrative step rail
- Sticky explanation canvas
- Interactive timelines
- Matching and weighting mini simulators
- Extraction cards for note-derived variables
- Clean plotted result bands and bias charts
